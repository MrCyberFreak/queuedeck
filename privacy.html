Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE DATE · 2026-07-12

QueueDeck is built local-first: the application runs on your computer, and your data stays there. This policy explains exactly what QueueDeck accesses, what it stores and where, what it transmits, and how you can remove all of it. It applies to the QueueDeck desktop application and this website.

1. The short version

  • QueueDeck has no user accounts, no analytics trackers, and no cloud storage. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.
  • Data from your connected TikTok account is fetched using TikTok's official interfaces, displayed to you, and stored only on your computer.
  • Content you compose is transmitted only to TikTok, and only when you publish it.
  • Disconnecting your account and deleting the app's local data folder removes everything.

2. Data the app accesses from your connected account

When you connect your TikTok account through TikTok's official login (Login Kit), QueueDeck receives a revocable access grant limited to the permissions you approve:

  • Profile basics (user.info.basic): display name and avatar, shown in the app so you can see which account is connected.
  • Account statistics (user.info.stats): follower, following, like, and video counts, shown in your analytics dashboard.
  • Video list and metrics (video.list): your public videos with their titles, covers, and view/like/comment/share counts, used to build your local analytics history.
  • Posting (video.upload, video.publish): used solely to transmit the posts you approve to TikTok.

QueueDeck never receives your TikTok password. Authentication happens on TikTok's own pages.

3. Where data is stored

  • On your computer: drafts and media you add, captions and post settings, your schedule, fetched analytics history, and the encrypted access/refresh tokens for your connected account. Default location: %APPDATA%\QueueDeck.
  • Nowhere else. QueueDeck operates no servers that store your content or account data. The developer cannot see your drafts, schedule, tokens, or analytics.

4. What the app transmits, and to whom

  • To TikTok: the posts you approve for publishing (video/photos, caption, and the settings you chose), and API requests needed to fetch your profile, stats, and video metrics. These go directly from your computer to TikTok's servers.
  • To no one else: QueueDeck contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and sends no telemetry.

5. This website

This site is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. It sets no cookies and runs no analytics scripts. GitHub, as the hosting provider, may log standard web-server request data (such as IP address) per GitHub's privacy statement. Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which receives the standard requests needed to serve font files.

6. Data retention and deletion

  • Tokens: deleted from your machine when you disconnect your account in the app. You can also revoke QueueDeck's access from your TikTok settings at any time, which invalidates the tokens immediately.
  • Everything else: your drafts, schedule, and analytics history are local files; uninstalling QueueDeck or deleting %APPDATA%\QueueDeck removes them permanently.
  • Because we hold no copy of your data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete on our side. For any privacy question or request, contact queuedeck.app@gmail.com.

7. Children

QueueDeck is not directed at children and may only be used by people who meet the connected platform's minimum age requirements.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the new version will be posted here with an updated effective date. Because the app is local-first, changes can never retroactively grant us access to data we never collected.
