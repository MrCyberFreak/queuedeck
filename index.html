Built around one idea: the queue is the strategy.

Consistency beats bursts. QueueDeck keeps a deck of ready-to-go content and releases it on the cadence you set — so a good week of production becomes a good month of posting.

Compose

A composer that respects the platform

Captions, cover frames, and every required setting in one pane: privacy level chosen by you (never defaulted), comment/duet/stitch controls that mirror your account's own settings, and a clear AI-generated-content label when you use one.

Queue

Slots, not stress

Define your weekly slots once — say Tue/Thu 19:00 and Sat noon. New approved posts fall into the next free slot automatically. Drag to reorder, pin a post to a date, or hold one back.

Publish

Direct publishing, verifiable status

Posts go out through the platform's official publishing interface. QueueDeck tracks each post from upload to live, shows processing status, and links you straight to the published post.

Analytics

Numbers without the spin

Your video list with views, likes, comments, and shares, pulled from the official data interface into clean local history — so you can see which formats and hooks actually carry.

Local-first

Your content stays on your machine

QueueDeck is a desktop app. Your drafts, schedule, and history live locally. Nothing is uploaded anywhere except the post you told it to publish, when you told it to publish.

Control

Connected by you, revocable by you

You connect your account through the platform's own login — QueueDeck never sees your password. Disconnect from inside the app or from your platform settings at any time.

How it works

Three moves. The deck does the remembering.

Connect your account

Sign in through the platform's official login page. QueueDeck requests only the permissions it needs: read your profile and video stats, upload, and publish.

Load the deck

Drop in finished videos or photo sets, write captions, set the required options, and approve each post. Approved posts join the queue.

Let the schedule run

QueueDeck publishes each post in its slot, confirms it went live, and folds the results back into your analytics view.

Compliance is a feature, not a checkbox.

Publishing tools should make the right thing the easy thing. QueueDeck implements the platform's content-sharing requirements to the letter:

  • Privacy chosen per post, from your account's own optionsNo pre-selected default — you decide every time.
  • Comment, Duet, and Stitch controls mirror your accountOptions your account disables are disabled here too.
  • Music Usage Confirmation shown before every publishYou confirm rights before anything goes out.
  • AI-generated content labelingOne switch marks a post as AI-generated where required.
  • Full preview and explicit consent before uploadNothing transfers until you hit Publish on the preview.
  • Commercial-content disclosure built inPromotional and branded-content flags where you need them.

Questions, answered straight

Is QueueDeck free?

QueueDeck is free while in early access. If that changes, existing users will get generous notice — and your data is local, so nothing gets held hostage.

Does QueueDeck post without my permission?

Never. Only posts you explicitly approved enter the queue, and the queue only runs while publishing is enabled. You can pause the whole deck with one switch.

What platforms does it support?

QueueDeck integrates with TikTok through TikTok's official developer interfaces. Support for additional short-video platforms is on the roadmap.

Does it need my password?

No. You sign in on the platform's own login page; QueueDeck receives a revocable access grant and never sees your credentials. Revoke it anytime from the app or your platform's settings.

Where does my data live?

On your computer. Drafts, media, schedule, and analytics history are stored locally. See the privacy policy for the full picture.
